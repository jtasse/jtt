Orbital Refuse Collector Demo

Interactive API Documentation with Satellite Orbit Visualization

This demo showcases an interactive visualization of the ORC (Orbital Refuse Collector) API. The scene features a monochromatic Earth with two satellites in different orbital patterns:

  • Geosynchronous Satellite (Blue) - Maintains a fixed position relative to Earth's surface
  • Non-Geosynchronous Satellite (Cyan) - Orbits at a different altitude with its own period

Use your mouse to rotate and zoom the view. Click the Home button to return to the main site.
